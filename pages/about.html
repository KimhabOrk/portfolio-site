EARLY LIFE

I was born with an innate sense of creativity and an eye for detail. My early years were marked by a keen interest in art and design, which set the foundation for my future career. The vibrant cultural heritage of my hometown played a significant role in shaping my aesthetic sensibilities. This upbringing laid the groundwork for my eventual foray into the fashion world.

EDUCATION

I pursued my higher education, focusing on integrating business principles with creative design. I graduated with a foundation degree in Business and Marketing from an institute in Singapore, SSTC Institute. However, my passion for fashion design led me to further my studies at Accademia Italiana in Italy, where I obtained a bachelor's degree in Fashion Design. This unique combination of business and art education equipped me with the skills to navigate the competitive fashion industry successfully.

CAREER

Currently, I share my knowledge as a lecturer at two prestigious universities, inspiring the next generation of fashion designers. My dual role as a designer and educator allows me to influence and inspire the future of fashion.

Beyond my professional achievements, I lead a fulfilling personal life. I enjoy exploring different cultures and find inspiration in my travels. My interest always influencing my designs. My personal experiences and interests greatly enrich my creative process and professional projects.
